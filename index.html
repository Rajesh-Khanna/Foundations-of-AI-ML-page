Mode of Teaching

Curriculum: There are 16 modules in the course. Each module comprises of 12 hours of live interactive sessions (relayed to centres from IIT Kharagpur).

Classes: The classes will be conducted on the weekends on mentioned centres, each Saturday and Sunday over 6 months, over 6 hour lectures.

Lecture Delivery:

  1. The program will be administered in the different centres across 5 cities.
  2. The classes will be conducted in the weekends (Saturday and Sunday). Timings shall be 9 AM to 4 PM on each day.
  3. The lectures will be given from one of the centres and will be delivered live over video con- ferencing with facilities of interaction with the students.
  4. There may be periodic contact of the students with the faculty at the different centres for discussion and doubt clearing.
  5. The classes will be recorded and made available to the registered students for viewing after the lecture.
  6. Course material will be made available to the students.
  7. Forums will be used for discussion among the students, faculty and TAs for each course module.

Support:

  1. Email support, for any academic query post sessions, where the faculty will revert to relevant queries.
  2. The program shall also utilize an Online Repository where additional materials, videos, assignments etc. will be hosted.
  3. Recorded videos of the classes shall be made available after the sessions for the benefits of the students.
  4. Learning Management System and platform access for hands-on practices shall be provided.

The selection of the students for the program will be based on: Online entrance test

The programme will be administered in different centres across the country

  • The classes will be conducted in the weekends (Saturday and Sunday). Tentative timings shall be 9 AM to 4 PM on each day.
  • The lectures will be given from one of the centres and will be delivered live over video conferencing with facilities of interaction with the students.
  • The classes will be recorded and made available to the registered students for viewing after the lecture and the same shall be available for at least one year.
  • Course material will be made available to the students
  • Forums will be used for discussion among the students, faculty and TAs for each course module
  • Every course will have tests and/or assignments. Students who pass will receive grades.

The instructors of the course will belong to one of the following groups:

  1. Faculty of IIT Kharagpur
  2. Guest Faculty under Centre for Artificial Intelligence
    1. Faculty members of other reputed academic institutes
    2. Industry professionals with extensive experience in the domain of Artificial Intelligence.

  3. The examinations shall be conducted online. For online examination the students are required to bring their laptops on the day of the examination to the centre and an online platform will be used for the examination.

  3. The certificate will be handed to the student through the Continuing Education Program IIT Kharagpur.
